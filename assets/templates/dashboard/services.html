Services

At Zentora Info Global Solutions, we provide a wide range of technology-driven services designed to enhance efficiency, security, and connectivity for businesses and individuals. Our GPS and Tracking Solutions enable real-time monitoring of vehicles and assets, ensuring complete visibility and control. With Video Telematics, we deliver intelligent video monitoring systems that improve fleet safety and operational insights. Our Billing Software with Bluetooth Printers simplifies transactions, providing fast, accurate, and seamless billing solutions for various industries. For the agricultural sector, our Farmer Security IoT Solutions protect crops, livestock, and farm assets through smart sensors and remote monitoring. We also specialize in custom software development, creating tailored web, mobile, and desktop applications to meet unique business needs. Complementing our products, we offer installation and technical support, ensuring smooth deployment and reliable maintenance. Additionally, our Security Systems (CCTV) provide comprehensive surveillance solutions, safeguarding homes, offices, and industrial facilities with high-quality monitoring and recording technology.
